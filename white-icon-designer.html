🎨 Creating Your White Icon

  1. Choose your favorite white/silver design above
  2. Screenshot or recreate in your design tool at 1024x1024px
  3. Color palette: White #ffffff, Silver #e0e0e0, Dark bg #1a1a1a
  4. Save as icon-1024.png (no transparency)
  5. Run: ./generate-app-icons.sh to create all sizes

💡 Design Notes

These white/silver designs provide a clean, modern aesthetic while maintaining the security theme. The contrast between the white lock and dark background creates strong visual impact, making the icon easily recognizable even at small sizes.

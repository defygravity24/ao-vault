🎨 Creating Your Vault Icon

  1. Choose your favorite vault-inspired design above
  2. Screenshot or recreate in your design tool at 1024x1024px
  3. Use the exact colors: Black background #0a0a0a and Gold #FFD700
  4. Save as icon-1024.png (no transparency)
  5. Run: ./generate-app-icons.sh to create all sizes

💡 Design Philosophy

These designs emphasize the "vault" concept - a secure, private place for your fanfiction collection. The gold (#FFD700) on black creates a premium, secure feeling that matches your app's aesthetic. The lock and vault imagery reinforces privacy and personal ownership of the library.

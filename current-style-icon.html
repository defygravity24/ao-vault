🎨 Creating Your Icon

  1. Choose the design that best matches your vision
  2. Create at 1024x1024px in your design tool
  3. Use subtle gradients for depth (not flat colors)
  4. Keep text readable even at small sizes

💡 Design Tips for App Store

  2. Test at 60px - Must be recognizable small
  3. Consistent with app - Match your app's UI
  4. Stand out - Light icons pop on dark App Store backgrounds
